Our Heritage

Founded in the cobbled streets of French Pondicherry, our maison has been crafting exceptional tobacco experiences for over a century. Each blend tells a story of colonial elegance and Tamil craftsmanship.

From the iconic Indian classics like Gold Flake and Scissors to the finest Cuban cigars, we curate only the most distinguished tobacco products for our discerning clientele.

— Pierre Dumas, Maître Tobacconist

LE PONDICHÉRY

Since 1892, we have been the premier destination for tobacco connoisseurs in South India. Our collection spans from heritage Indian brands to the world's most exclusive cigars.
